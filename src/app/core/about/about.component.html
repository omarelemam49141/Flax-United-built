About Flax United Import and Export

Flax United Import and Export is a distinguished leader in the global linen industry, proudly representing Egypt as the sole company dedicated to producing and exporting premium linen products. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability has earned us a renowned reputation worldwide.

Based in the heart of Egypt, Flax United specializes in the cultivation, processing, and export of high-grade flax fibers. Our expertise spans from the finest long-fiber flax to versatile short-fiber products, catering to diverse industries and markets.

With a deep-rooted heritage and a forward-thinking approach, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver products that meet the highest international standards. Our unwavering dedication to excellence has made us a trusted partner for clients around the globe, ensuring that Flax United remains at the forefront of the linen industry.

At Flax United, we are not just exporting linen; we are exporting the legacy of Egyptian craftsmanship to the world.
